Who are we

We live with the community

We promote a low stress community atmosphere, where we can take our time, think before we act, and have fun together! We respect each other and our differences. We act and communicate responsibly and take responsibility for our actions in game. It is a server free from griefing, thieving and moderators.

We aim for a simple yet enjoyable server. If there are suggestions made by any player we will take a look at them and maybe they might be implemented!

Specifications

Processor

An octa-core processor reaching up to 5Ghz gives us seamless gameplay. Having a processor that can keep up with the server reduces the amount of lag spikes and rubber banding our players encounter.

Storage

Despite our quality specifications, the world of technology can be unpredictable. Although these unexpected occurrences are rare, we are still prepared with 1TB of SSD storage that allows bi-hourly back-ups of our world. Should any corruption occur, we can restore lost or corrupted data quickly and minimize the effect it could have on our players.

RAM

With 8GB of dedicated RAM for the server, our players don't have to worry about random server crashes that could result in loss of data and can enjoy a hassle-free environment.

When running a multiplayer server like ours, speed and resources are everthing. The Near Vanilla server and website run on a dedicated, Linux based server that is equipped and designed to give players the best play experience possible on a butter-smooth server.

General Information

Donations

The pay-to-win game structure is annoying. You already own Minecraft, so why would you want to pay in order to access a certain aspect of game play? On Near Vanilla, this doesn’t exist. 100% of all donations are put toward server costs and no in-game items will be given for donations. Several members enjoy this aspect of the server and donate to simply assist with server costs, for which we are very grateful.
Our donation store can be found here.

Discord

Near Vanilla uses Discord as our main source of communication. Discord is an all-in-one voice and text chat for gamers that's free, secure, and works on both your desktop and phone. As an official Discord partner we hope you do yourself the favor of getting Discord and really become a part of the community. The people here are friendly and will be happy to give you a tour of things on the server. Lastly, we have our in-game Minecraft chat integrated through Discord, making it quick and easy to communicate with players at all times.

DeathLog

Always know where exactly you died.
No F3 screen required!

DeathLog

Say Goodbye to watching ugly F3 screen in case a creeper sneaks up on you. Your death coordinates will be sent to you in a private message each time you die.

Dynmap

A Google Maps-like map for our Minecraft server that can be viewed in a browser.

Dynmap

Dynmap allows us to create beautiful maps of our server, so that you always know where you going.

CapAFK

A simple yet core plugin that allows us to kick AFK players.

CapAFK

Once the player has been playing for 3 hours, the player will have 60 seconds to type in a capatcha. If the player can't do that, then the player gets kicked from the server.

CoreProtect

CoreProtect is a fast, efficient, data logging and anti-griefing tool.

CoreProtect

Rollback and restore any amount of damage. Designed with large servers in mind, CoreProtect will record and manage data without impacting our server's performance. Thanks to it, we don't need claim plugins and are able to seamlessly protect our players.

DiscordSRV

Powerful, configurable, open-source Discord to Minecraft bridging plugin.

DiscordSRV

Using this plugin, we are able to give players the ability to chat in-game to chat with players on our Discord server as well as having people on the Discord server be able to chat with people on the server

One Player Sleep

Have the bed just for yourself.
We don't judge!

One Player Sleep

Sometimes people AFK for a moment, or maybe they are away from bed. This plugin allows for skipping the night, as long as at least one player is in bed and noone wants to keep the night.

isSlimeChunk

A simple plugin show chunk is Slime chunk or not.

isSlimeChunk

This plugin will display in chat if the chunk you're standing on is a slime chunk or not. This way you don't need the seed.
Use the following command:
/slime?

LuckPerms

Dialing just right level of permission for all of our players.

LuckPerms

We can easily manage all of our players and give everyone all the permissions they need.

WorldBorder

This is basic card with image on top, title, description and button.

WorldBorder

This plugin is intended to efficiently provide a border for each of your worlds, which only people granted special bypass access are allowed beyond. These passses can be bought in our donation shop.
